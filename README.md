# AI Access Lab: A Databricks Project for Democratizing AI Learning

AI Access Lab is a beginner-friendly Databricks project designed to show how a campus AI/ML club could use data to understand student learning barriers, design better workshops, and teach responsible AI workflows.

## Project Goal

The goal is to make AI and data science feel more accessible to students who may be curious but intimidated by coding, unfamiliar with machine learning, or unsure how AI connects to their interests.

## What This Project Demonstrates

- Synthetic data generation
- Data cleaning and validation
- PySpark DataFrame workflows
- SQL analysis
- Data visualization
- Simple machine learning with scikit-learn
- MLflow experiment tracking
- Responsible AI reflection
- A beginner-friendly workshop plan

## Notebooks

4. `04_workshop_plan_and_reflection`

## Why Databricks?

Databricks provides a unified environment for data engineering, analytics, and machine learning. This project uses Databricks Free Edition as a learning platform to help students understand the full data-to-AI workflow, not just the final model.

## Campus Impact

This project is designed to become a workshop for Smith students through an AI/ML club. It is especially focused on students who are new to AI, students from communities with less exposure to machine learning, and students who want a more grounded understanding of what AI systems actually involve.
